About Us
The Catholic Newman Club at Robert Morris University is a faith-centered community dedicated to helping students grow spiritually, intellectually, and socially while living out the teachings of the Catholic Church on campus. All are welcome.
History
Founded in 2010, the Catholic Newman Club at Robert Morris University supports students through prayer, fellowship, service, and faith formation.
